Instructions

  • Move tiles around by dragging and dropping.
  • The Shuffle button shuffles the tiles on the rack.
  • The Exchange button allows you to exchange some/all of your tiles (this costs you your turn).
  • The Recall button brings back all the unsubmitted tiles on the board back to the rack
  • Double-clicking an unsubmitted tile will bring it back to the rack
  • Points possible shows the points you could earn assuming your words are in the dictionary
  • The Play button submits your tiles.
  • A list of two-letter words and a dictionary interface are provided for convenience.
