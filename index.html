Scrabble

A pass-and-play scrabble game implementation.

Instructions

  • Enter Player Names (at least two required), choose whether to randomize play order, and decide type of game.
  • If dictionary checking is on, each submitted word will be checked against the official Scrabble dictionary

Once game starts

  • Move tiles around by dragging and dropping.
  • The Shuffle button shuffles the tiles on the rack.
  • The Exchange button allows you to exchange some/all of your tiles (this costs you your turn).
  • The Recall button brings back all tiles on the board to the rack
  • Double-clicking a tile will bring it back to the rack
  • The Play button submits your tiles.
  • A list of two-letter words and a dictionary interface are provided for convenience.
